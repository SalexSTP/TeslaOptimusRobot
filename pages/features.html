Features And Specs

Humanoid Design: Optimus is about 5 feet 8 inches tall and weighs approximately 125 pounds, with a sleek, human-like appearance to navigate environments designed for humans.

Safety: It is designed to move at a maximum speed of 5 mph to ensure safety when interacting with humans.

Human-Aid Focus: Its primary purpose is to handle repetitive or menial tasks, like lifting items, assembling components, or even helping with household chores.

AI Integration: It leverages Tesla’s advanced AI, initially developed for self-driving cars, allowing it to recognize objects, navigate spaces, and interact with its surroundings intelligently.

Mobility: Optimus features 40+ mechanical actuators for movement and can lift heavy objects (up to 45 lbs) and carry smaller weights over long distances.

Tesla’s Vision: Elon Musk has described Optimus as a significant step toward reducing physical labor and creating a future where humans can focus on more creative and fulfilling tasks.
